Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. phraise it collects and stores only the personal information you provide within the extension for its intended purpose, such as improving text and autofilling forms. This data includes:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Address

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties. You can delete or update your information at any time by accessing the "Details Form" section in the extension.

We use the OpenAI API to process and improve your text. The text you enter in the extension is sent securely to the API and is not stored by us. For more details, please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
